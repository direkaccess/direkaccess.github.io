Acron Group and VTB Bank (Austria) AG pursue their cooperation

05.05.2011 - Acron Group, Russia, a leading manufacturer of a broad range of fertilisers, and VTB Bank (Austria) AG today signed a US$ 175 million term loan facility with a tenor of 5 years. This PXF facility supports the long term financing structure of the group and further enhances its financial flexibility.

“We are persuaded of Acron’s further path and are pleased to support the company’s long term financial strategy. This further facility is evidence of many years of mutual beneficial cooperation with Acron Group,” – commented Igor Strehl, CEO of VTB Bank (Austria) AG.
