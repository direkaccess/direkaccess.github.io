Facts & Figures

VTB Group is a global financial group whose core business focus is in the areas of Corporate, Retail and Investment Banking.

VTB Bank in Moscow is the heart of the Group. Stock in VTB Bank is listed on the MICEX and RTS and traded on the London Stock Exchange in the form of global depository receipts. International ratings agencies Moody's Investors Services, Fitch and Standard & Poor's have given VTB the highest ratings possible for Russian banks.

May 2007

  • Initial public offering of the Bank
  • Russian and international investors acquire 22.5% of VTB shares
  • With a volume of shares traded at almost $ 8.0 billion, the largest IPO in Europe for the year 2007
  • In Russia, 120,000 citizens become VTB shareholders.

2009

  • Russian government increases its share in VTB Bank AG to 85.5% of share capital.

2011/2012

  • 10 % of the shares of VTB Bank are brought onto the capital market.
  • Acquisition of TransCreditBank and Bank Moscow

2013

  • Launch of Leto Bank
  • Privatisation program implemented

2014

  • The State's share in the Bank falls to 60.9%.
