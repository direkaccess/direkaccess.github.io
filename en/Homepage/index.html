VTB Bank (Austria) AG - expanding your business horizon!

With our head office in Vienna and our subsidiaries VTB Bank (Deutschland) AG in Frankfurt and VTB Bank (France) SA in Paris, we are the European Subholding of VTB Bank.

For a name you can trust when you're looking to expand your business, talk to us! Our experienced experts give you the strongest support in your border-crossing business dealings.

We are focusing on the individual details of your business, providing bespoke, sophisticated solutions that perfectly suit your organization's needs.
