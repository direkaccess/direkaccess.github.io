Capital resources & subsidies

Whether public funding schemes or equity capital, we advise our customers holistically within the scope of their projects and develop the suitable financing solution on the basis of their individual profiles. Taking into account their financial position and earnings situation and industry-specific requirements, we create a strengths-and-weaknesses profile and work out the appropriate financing solution together.

Public funding schemes can provide long-term financing at tangibly reduce interest expense for investments and often lessen financing costs through government grants. Our specialists in public funding schemes  analyze potential aid options early on in the investment planning process and develop financing concepts on this basis.

The main areas in which we advise clients are public funding schemes for SMEs, for environmental protection and energy efficiency, for renewable energies, innovations and municipal infrastructure investment projects. Here, we have held a leading position in the Austria market for decades. Our employees have comprehensive knowledge and many years of experience when it comes to incorporating development funds.

The public funding schemes are provided by government and state level development banks in addition to European development agencies. Our most important refinancing partner among the development banks is KfW.
