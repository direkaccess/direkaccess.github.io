Financial Sponsors

We maintain long-term relationships with the private equity community throughout Europe. We have an active dialogue with the majority of financial sponsors active in our focus regions DACH, France, Italy, Benelux, and selectively Nordics and CEE. We are covering a broad range of financial sponsors across all deal and fund size brackets, including small-, medium-, large-cap buy-out funds, special situation, real estate and infrastructure funds as well as family offices. Together with IKB’s dedicated industry and product teams we provide financial sponsors and their portfolio companies with customized financial solutions and advisory services. The service offering comprises the entire corporate finance product suite including merger & acquisition advisory, leveraged buy-out financings and recapitalizations as well as equity and debt capital markets products (advisory/structuring/execution).

Our clients benefit from the team’s in-depth and long-term market experience as well as from our exceptional access into Austria Mittelstand companies to develop and execute value generating and often times investment ideas, both majorities and minorities.

Since 2009, the team has successfully executed more than 100 transactions.
