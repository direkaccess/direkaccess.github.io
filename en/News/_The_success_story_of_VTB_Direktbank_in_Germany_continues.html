The success story of VTB Direktbank in Germany continues

For VTB Direktbank the year 2014 was marked by the winning of multiple awards and the introduction of three new products. Since its launch in 2011 in Germany, VTB Direktbank has continually pursued a successful course.

 

VTB Direktbank received multiple awards

In 2014 VTB Direktbank, a Germany-based branch of VTB Bank (Austria) AG, has received multiple awards for its excellent services and innovative products on the German online banking market.

“The numerous awards received by VTB Direktbank are a clear proof that we have successfully managed to gain a strong position in the German private banking market. We are eager to continue this way in 2015”, says Jan-Peter Kind, Managing Director of VTB Direktbank. Among all the prizes received, the Zinsaward 2014 in the category “best long-term deposit” and the 2nd rank in the category “long-term deposit” in Germany’s major bank test are of the utmost importance for VTB Direktbank. To see the full list of awards and distinctions granted to VTB Direktbank, please visit their homepage at http://www.vtbdirekt.de/de/News_und_Presse/Auszeichnungen/.


Three successful new products

Aside from winning multiple awards, VTB Direktbank has successfully introduced three new products this year. The existing online product portfolio was enlarged by the launch of the VTB pay out plan, the VTB online savings account and the VTB Young products. These products address the needs of three distinct target groups, however, what they all have in common is excellent service quality and attractive interest rates. Again a further proof that VTB Direktbank maintains its successful course.


VTB Direktbank at a glance

VTB Direktbank is a Germany-based branch of VTB Bank (Austria) AG. Being a cost-efficient online bank, VTB Direktbank focuses on simple, attractive and secure investment products for retail clients in Germany. Visit the homepage of VTB Direktbank at http://www.vtbdirektbank.de to learn more about the product portfolio and services.
